FAQ'S

What is $FluffCoin (FLUFFY)?
$FluffCoin is the cutest and fluffiest meme coin on the Solana blockchain. It's a community-driven project dedicated to creating a fun, engaging ecosystem while supporting dog-related charitable causes worldwide.
When was $FluffCoin founded?
$FluffCoin was founded in July 2024 with the mission to bring joy, support charitable contributions, and foster a strong, engaged community within the crypto world.
What makes $FluffCoin unique?
$FluffCoin combines the light-hearted nature of memes with the serious potential of blockchain technology. We focus on community engagement, charitable initiatives, and financial opportunities for our holders.
How does $FluffCoin support charitable causes?
10% of our total supply is allocated to supporting dog-related causes globally. We partner with various dog charities to provide funding for shelters, treatments, and adoption programs.
What is the total supply of $FluffCoin?
The total supply of $FluffCoin is 1,000,000,000 FLUFFY tokens.
How are the $FluffCoin tokens distributed?
Community Rewards: 10% (100,000,000 FLUFFY)
Development and Marketing: 10% (100,000,000 FLUFFY)
Charity and Donations: 10% (100,000,000 FLUFFY)
Liquidity Pool: 15% (150,000,000 FLUFFY)
Team Allocation: 10% (100,000,000 FLUFFY)
Ecosystem Growth: 45% (450,000,000 FLUFFY)
What security measures are in place for $FluffCoin?
$FluffCoin has achieved KYC certification through Assure DeFi, ensuring trust and credibility within our community. We also have locked liquidity to ensure the stability and security of our token.
What are the future plans for $FluffCoin?
Our roadmap includes:
- Expanded listings on more decentralized exchanges.
- Launch of staking rewards once the community grows.
- Implementation of multi-language support for global outreach.
- Increased support for dog-related charitable causes.

How can I participate in $FluffCoin events and AMAs?
We regularly host AMAs and community events. Stay tuned to our Telegram and Twitter channels for announcements and updates on upcoming events.
What should I do if I encounter issues or have questions about $FluffCoin?
For any issues or questions, please contact us at info@fluffcoinfluffy.com. We are here to help and ensure a smooth experience for all our community members.

$FluffCoin is dedicated to creating a fun, impactful, and community-driven project. Join us on this fluffy adventure and help us make the crypto world a better place, one $FluffCoin at a time!
