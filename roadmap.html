Staking Rewards

As part of our commitment to providing value to our community, $FluffCoin plans to introduce a staking rewards program in 2024. This program will allow $FluffCoin holders to stake their tokens and earn additional rewards, promoting long-term holding and community engagement.

How It Works:

  • Eligibility: $FluffCoin holders will be able to participate in the staking program by locking their tokens in a dedicated staking contract.
  • Rewards Distribution: Stakers will earn rewards in the form of additional $FluffCoin tokens, distributed proportionally based on the number of tokens staked and the duration of the staking period.
  • Incentives: This initiative aims to incentivize long-term investment in $FluffCoin, contributing to the stability and growth of the project.

Stay tuned for more detailed information on how to participate in the staking rewards program as we move closer to its launch.
